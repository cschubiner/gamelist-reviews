# CapRiders: Euro Soccer

## Game Overview
CapRiders: Euro Soccer is an arcade online and local multiplayer soccer game featuring caricatures of famous European football players. Players control individual footballers and compete on unique stadiums with special twists and peculiarities.

## Platforms
- PC (Steam)

## Player Count
- **Local Multiplayer**: Up to 22 players
- **Online Multiplayer**: Up to 8 players
- Supports up to 8 gamepads or mobile devices (iOS/Android) via downloadable app

## Pricing
Available on Steam with frequent sales (75% discount reported on various key sites)

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Each player takes control of a single footballer and competes on various unique stadiums including:
- Flipper Arena
- Pool Palace
- Circle Garden
- Infinite Stadium
- And more

Players can challenge friends in 2v2 or 3v3 matches on the Competitive Arena. The game features 12 European teams including Germany, England, France, Italy, Spain, Sweden, Portugal, and Belgium, each with lineups inspired by famous football players.

Control options include traditional gamepads or using iOS/Android phones and tablets as controllers through a dedicated app available on the App Store and Play Store.
