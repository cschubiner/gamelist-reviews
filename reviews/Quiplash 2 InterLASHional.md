# Quiplash 2 InterLASHional

## Overview
Quiplash 2 InterLASHional is the standalone multilingual version of Quiplash 2 from The Jackbox Party Pack 3. This "say anything" party game challenges 3-8 players to write the funniest responses to prompts, then vote on each other's answers. It was the first Jackbox party game to be localized in multiple languages (French, Italian, German, and Spanish), with 100 new prompts in each language.

## Gameplay & Mechanics

### Core Gameplay
- Players receive prompts on their devices
- Type in creative, funny responses
- Head-to-head battles pit answers against each other
- Other players and audience vote on favorites
- Points awarded based on votes received
- Funniest answers win

### Prompt System
- Hundreds of prompts included
- 100 new prompts per language (French, Italian, German, Spanish)
- Wide variety of topics and situations
- Encourages creative and humorous thinking

### Make Your Own Feature
- Create custom prompts
- Personalize the game for your group
- Added in June 19, 2020 update
- Initially absent, added based on player feedback

### Player Roles
- **Active Players**: 3-8 players writing answers
- **Audience**: Unlimited spectators can vote
- **Voting**: Everyone participates in selecting winners

### Device Integration
- Players use smartphones, tablets, or computers
- No special controllers needed
- Simple web-based interface
- Easy setup and joining

## Fun Factor
Quiplash 2 InterLASHional is consistently one of the most entertaining Jackbox games. The simple premise of "write something funny" creates hilarious moments, especially with the right group. The head-to-head format builds tension, and seeing how friends interpret prompts differently generates genuine laughter. Perfect for parties, family gatherings, or online hangouts. The game scales well from minimum to maximum players.

## Player Count Experience

- **Audience Mode**: Unlimited spectators can vote and participate
- **Minimum**: 3 players (functional but limited)
- **Sweet Spot**: 5-8 players for best experience
- **Remote Play**: Excellent for online parties via streaming
