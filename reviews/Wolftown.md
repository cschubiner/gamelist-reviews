# Wolftown

## Overview
Wolftown is a 4 to 16 player local multiplayer social deduction game based on the classic Werewolf (aka Mafia) party game. Players connect to the game using their smartphone devices, and the game's narrator and colorful graphics lead players through a tale of murder, intrigue, and betrayal.

## Gameplay & Mechanics

### Core Gameplay
- Social deduction mechanics based on Werewolf/Mafia
- Smartphone device integration for player input
- Interactive narrator system
- Colorful graphics to enhance the experience

### Day and Night Phases
**Night Phase:**
- Players secretly selected as werewolves eliminate townspeople
- Hidden roles and secret actions

**Day Phase:**
- Everyone gathers to discuss and argue
- Vote to eliminate one player
- Hopefully identify a wolf, but possibly eliminate an innocent

### Role System
- Multiple secret roles available
- Roles carefully selected and tweaked to maximize interplay
- Full interactive tutorial included
- Complete reference guide for all secret roles

### Game Pace
- Fast-paced gameplay
- Typical game lasts about 15 minutes (depending on player count)
- Eliminated players still given tasks to keep them engaged
- No long waiting periods for eliminated players

## Fun Factor
Wolftown takes the classic social deduction formula and enhances it with modern technology integration. The smartphone connectivity makes it easy to play without physical cards or complicated setup. The quick pace and continued engagement of eliminated players helps maintain fun throughout the entire game session.

## Player Count Experience
