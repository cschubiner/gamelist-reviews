# Christmas Movie Bingo

## Game Information
- **Developer:** Independent
- **Publisher:** Independent
- **Platforms:** Steam (Windows), Web Browser
- **Release Date:** 2020
- **Price:** $5.00 (Steam estimate); Physical versions vary ($16.99 for Hallmark official pad)
- **Player Count:** Unlimited (all viewers can play simultaneously)
- **Online Play:** Supports Zoom and remote play

## Overview
Christmas Movie Bingo generates a bingo card for each person watching with Christmas movie tropes in each space. The goal is to be the first player to notice enough clichés to fill 5-in-a-row. The game is designed to enhance the experience of watching made-for-TV Christmas movies, particularly the formulaic romantic comedies that dominate holiday programming.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Players can use phones, tablets, or laptops to access their bingo cards. The game works over Zoom, making it perfect for remote holiday gatherings when families can't be together physically. In a single game, all players have the same 24 spaces on their bingo card, but subsequent games might feature different sets of tropes.

Spaces typically include Christmas movie clichés like:
- Big city career woman returns to small hometown
- Handsome widower with adorable child
- A bakery or coffee shop is in danger of closing
- Someone discovers the "true meaning of Christmas"
- Convenient snowfall at romantic moments
- Christmas tree decorating scene
- Hot chocolate with marshmallows

Players mark off spaces as they spot tropes during the movie, racing to be first to complete a line.

## Fun Factor
Christmas Movie Bingo transforms predictable holiday movies into interactive entertainment. It's particularly enjoyable with Hallmark-style movies known for following strict formulas. The game gently pokes fun at holiday movie conventions without being mean-spirited, maintaining the cozy festive atmosphere while adding competitive excitement.

The interactive element makes even the most formulaic Christmas movies more engaging, and calling out tropes becomes part of the fun. It's especially popular for movie marathons where the repetitive nature of holiday films might otherwise become tedious.

## Player Count Experience
With unlimited simultaneous players, Christmas Movie Bingo works for intimate family movie nights or large holiday parties. Everyone stays engaged throughout the entire film rather than waiting for turns. The format is particularly valuable for maintaining connection during virtual holiday gatherings via Zoom.
