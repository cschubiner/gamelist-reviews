# Ropeat

## Overview
Ropeat is a fun top-down shooter game designed for 2-10 players where each player controls a robot with a unique repeat-mechanic. Players get copies that repeat exactly what they did a few seconds ago, creating strategic gameplay possibilities.

## Gameplay & Mechanics

### Core Gameplay
- Top-down shooter perspective
- Unique repeat mechanic where actions are replayed by copies
- Players must strategize around their past actions
- Robot-based combat

### Player Actions
- Control your robot in real-time
- Your actions are recorded and repeated by copies
- Plan moves carefully as past actions affect future strategy
- Use the repeat mechanic to your advantage in combat

## Fun Factor
The innovative repeat mechanic adds a unique twist to the top-down shooter genre. Players must think ahead and use their recorded actions strategically, creating interesting gameplay dynamics and opportunities for creative tactics.
