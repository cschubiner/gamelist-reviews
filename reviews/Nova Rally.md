# Nova Rally

## Overview
Nova Rally is described as "the chaos-fuelled, stats-driven party racer that blends strategic depth, outrageous action, and the thrill of live competition." It's a chaotic party racer for up to 24 players where players assume the role of a pit manager and command their race team.

**Note:** There are two different games with the Nova Rally name - a party racer on Steam and a blockchain-based game on WAX. This review focuses on the party racer version.

## Gameplay & Mechanics

### Core Gameplay (Party Racer Version)
- Stats-based racing combined with real-time decisions
- Players act as pit managers commanding their race team
- Strategic depth meets outrageous action
- Live competition elements

### Player Management
- Command and manage your race team
- Make real-time strategic decisions
- Balance stats and tactical choices
- Secure victory through smart management

### Multiplayer
- Up to 24 players per game
- Party racer atmosphere
- Chaotic competitive gameplay

### Blockchain Version (Alternative)
- Play-to-Earn mechanics on WAX Blockchain
- Pick 1 car and 2 rally drivers
- Race results generated instantly
- NOVA TALLY game engine calculates winners

## Fun Factor
The party racer version emphasizes chaotic fun with large player counts and strategic team management. The blend of stats-driven racing and real-time decision-making creates a unique party racing experience that goes beyond simple driving mechanics.
