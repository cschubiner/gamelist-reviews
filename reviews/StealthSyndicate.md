# Stealth Syndicate

## Overview
Stealth Syndicate is a party game that blends stealth, speed, and tactics, where players must stay hidden among crowds while attempting to defeat opponents before getting spotted. Released on November 18, 2024, this indie action game supports both local and online multiplayer for 2 to 10 players across 7 different game modes.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Stealth Syndicate combines elements of social deduction with fast-paced action gameplay. Players must use a mix of stealth mechanics, quick reflexes, and strategic thinking to stay concealed while hunting their targets. The game's core loop involves blending in with crowds, identifying targets, and executing eliminations without being detected.

With 7 different game modes, the game offers variety in how players approach each match. The Target mode specifically tasks all players with finding and eliminating a designated target each round, creating tension as everyone races to complete their objective first while avoiding detection.

The game supports both local and online play, allowing flexibility in how groups want to experience the social deduction and stealth elements. The 2-10 player range makes it scalable for different group sizes, from intimate duels to larger party settings.

## Fun Factor
As a party game focused on social deduction and stealth mechanics, Stealth Syndicate offers the kind of suspenseful, "who can I trust" gameplay that makes for memorable moments with friends. The variety of game modes helps keep the experience fresh across multiple sessions, and the stealth mechanics add a layer of skill to the social deduction formula.

The tension of trying to identify and eliminate targets while remaining undetected creates engaging cat-and-mouse dynamics. Success requires not just quick reflexes but also careful observation and tactical decision-making.
