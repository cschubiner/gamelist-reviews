# Drawful 2

## Game Overview
Drawful 2 is the game of terrible drawings and hilariously wrong answers from Jackbox Games. Players draw prompts on their phones or tablets, others guess what the drawings are, and everyone votes on what they think is the correct answer.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
- Players receive ridiculous prompts and must draw them on their phone/tablet
- Other players create fake answers for what they think the drawing represents
- Everyone votes on which answer they believe is correct
- Points are awarded for fooling other players and guessing correctly
- Custom prompts can be created for personalized gameplay

## Fun Factor
Drawful 2 excels at creating hilarious moments through deliberately bad drawings and creative fake answers. The game's strength lies in its simplicity and accessibility - anyone can play regardless of artistic ability. The humor naturally emerges from the combination of awful drawings and clever misdirection. Custom prompts add significant replay value and allow groups to tailor the experience to their inside jokes and preferences.

## Player Count Experience
- **Supported Players**: 3-8 players
- **Audience Mode**: Thousands of additional players can participate
- **Optimal Experience**: 5-8 players provides the best balance of variety in answers while keeping rounds moving
- **Phone/Tablet Required**: Each player needs their own device to draw and answer
