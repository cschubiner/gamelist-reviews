# RiffTrax: The Game

## Game Overview
RiffTrax: The Game is a multiplayer party experience where players compete to make bad movies funny, inspired by the comedy troupe behind Mystery Science Theater 3000. Players watch terrible movie clips and create or select humorous dialogue and commentary, with the goal of making the funniest riffs that will make everyone laugh.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
- Players watch clips from intentionally bad movies
- Create or select funny dialogue and commentary for scenes
- Vote on the funniest riffs from other players
- Head-to-head competition for up to 6 active players
- Up to 12 additional audience members can participate
- Similar gameplay style to What the Dub and Quiplash
- Twitch streaming integration for even larger audiences
- Multiple rounds with different clips and riffing opportunities

## Fun Factor
RiffTrax: The Game captures the joy of making fun of bad movies with friends. The pre-selected bad movie clips provide perfect setup for comedy, and players get to unleash their inner comedian. Works wonderfully for fans of MST3K and RiffTrax, but also appeals to anyone who enjoys comedic party games. The audience participation means even those waiting their turn stay engaged. Best with groups who appreciate irreverent humor and quick wit. Multiple rounds keep energy high, and the variety of clips prevents repetition.
