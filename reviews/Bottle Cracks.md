# Bottle Cracks

## Overview
Bottle Cracks is a simple yet challenging party game that has gained a strong following for its addictive gameplay and excellent multiplayer features. The game can be played both locally and online, making it accessible for various play situations.

## Gameplay & Mechanics

### Core Gameplay
- Simple concept executed very well
- Easy to learn but hard to master
- Challenging difficulty that keeps players engaged
- Straightforward mechanics with depth

### Multiplayer Features
- Local multiplayer support
- Online multiplayer functionality
- Minimal technical issues
- Seamless multiplayer experience

## Fun Factor
Players consistently describe Bottle Cracks as "SUCH A GREAT GAME" and "such a hilarious time with friends." The game's simple mechanics combined with genuine challenge create an engaging experience. While it can be played solo, the multiplayer aspect significantly enhances the fun factor. Many players have reported spending over 30 hours on the game, describing it as "annoying but addictive."

## Player Count Experience
- **Solo Play**: Fun but limited compared to multiplayer
- **Multiplayer**: The game truly shines with friends
- **Local & Online**: Both options available without problems
- **Party Game**: Perfect for group sessions
