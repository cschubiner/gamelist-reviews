# Verse Surf

## Overview
Verse Surf is a party game where players prove their music knowledge and bluffing skills. Whether you're a 60s rolling stone, 70s disco queen, 80s big hair rocker, or a walking music database, this game tests your ability to deceive friends with fake song lyrics. It's the developers' first PC and Steam game, created mainly in their spare time.

## Gameplay & Mechanics

### Core Gameplay
- Listen to song samples
- Write fake lyrics as if they're real
- Submit your lies to other players
- Gain points when friends vote for your fake lyrics
- Test music knowledge and creativity

### Bluffing and Deception
- Create convincing fake lyrics
- Trick opponents into believing your lies
- Music trivia meets social deduction
- Rewards creativity and music knowledge

### Controller Setup
- Use phones or tablets as game controllers
- No additional controllers needed
- Easy setup for party situations
- Accessible technology requirements

### Remote Play Features
- Play using Steam Remote Play
- Compatible with screen and sound sharing software
- Share with friends online
- No physical gathering required

## Fun Factor
Verse Surf combines music trivia with bluffing gameplay similar to popular party games like Jackbox titles. The phone/tablet controller system makes it easy for anyone to join. Best enjoyed with friends who appreciate music and humor. The game works well for both local and remote play sessions, making it versatile for different social situations.
