# Council of Mages: The Party Game

## Game Overview
Council of Mages is a fantasy-themed multiplayer party game where up to 6 friends compete to join the prestigious Council of Mages. Players customize their mage characters and participate in easy-to-learn mini-games, choosing to team up cooperatively or challenge each other competitively as they prove their magical prowess.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
- Customize your mage character with various options
- Collection of easy-to-learn mini-games
- Both cooperative and competitive game modes
- Supports up to 6 simultaneous players
- Multiple controller options: gamepads or smartphones
- Shared-screen local multiplayer focus
- Party game structure with rotating mini-games
- Fantasy theme with magical abilities and challenges

## Fun Factor
Council of Mages combines accessible mini-game gameplay with charming fantasy aesthetics. The character customization adds personal investment before the games begin. The variety between cooperative and competitive modes keeps groups engaged by switching up dynamics. Easy-to-learn mini-games mean new players can jump in quickly. The magical theme provides flavor without overwhelming the straightforward party game mechanics. Best for groups who enjoy mini-game collections like Mario Party but with a fantasy twist.
