# The Jackbox Party Pack 3 Review

## Overview
The Jackbox Party Pack 3 is widely considered one of the best entries in the Jackbox series. Released with five diverse party games, it offers a strong mix of trivia, creativity, deception, and humor that has made it a fan favorite since its release.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
The pack includes five varied games:
- **Quiplash 2** (3-8 players): The sequel to the popular head-to-head battle of wits with more prompts and the coveted "Thumbs Cup" award for especially clever answers
- **Trivia Murder Party** (1-8 players): A darkly comedic trivia game where wrong answers lead to deadly mini-games
- **Guesspionage** (2-8 players): A percentage-guessing game where players estimate survey results
- **Tee K.O.** (3-8 players): Players create drawings and slogans that get mixed, matched, and voted on as t-shirt designs
- **Fakin' It** (3-6 players): A social deduction game where players must identify the faker who doesn't know what the others are doing

Each game uses smartphones or tablets as controllers, making setup simple and accessible.

## Fun Factor
Critics and players consistently praise Party Pack 3 as "better than ever with a sequel to a fan favourite, a Family Fortunes-style quiz and perhaps the best game Jackbox have ever created." The variety ensures that different group dynamics and preferences are well-served.

Some reviewers noted that while production values have increased, substance may have decreased slightly compared to earlier packs, with gameplay sometimes feeling like "the unevenness of a book of Mad Libs." However, the overall package is still highly entertaining.

## Player Count Experience
- **Active Players:** 1-8 players (varies by game)
- **Audience:** Up to 10,000 spectators who can participate
- **Best For:** Groups of 3-8 players

The audience feature is particularly valuable for larger gatherings or streaming, allowing non-players to influence outcomes and stay engaged.

## Overall Reception

### Critical Scores
- **Steambase Player Score:** 97/100 (Overwhelmingly Positive)
- **Total Reviews:** 6,426 on Steam
- **Critical Consensus:** Highly positive, considered one of the best Party Packs

### Strengths
- Excellent variety across five distinct game types
- Contains several of the series' most beloved games
- Trivia Murder Party is frequently cited as one of Jackbox's best creations
- Strong replay value with different game modes
- Accessible to players of all skill levels
- Great balance of competitive and creative gameplay
- Fakin' It provides a unique social deduction experience
- Thumbs Cup in Quiplash 2 rewards especially clever humor

### Weaknesses
- A couple of games may not appeal to all groups
- Some feel production value increased while substance slightly decreased
- Can feel uneven like "Mad Libs" at times
- Requires multiple players for most games to shine

## Verdict
The Jackbox Party Pack 3 earns its reputation as one of the series' strongest entries. With the introduction of Trivia Murder Party (often considered Jackbox's best game), an improved Quiplash 2, and the unique social deduction of Fakin' It, it offers something for virtually every type of party game enthusiast. The overwhelmingly positive Steam rating and strong critical reception reflect its enduring appeal. For those new to Jackbox or looking to expand their collection, Party Pack 3 is an essential purchase.
