# Paper Pirates

## Game Overview
Paper Pirates is a team-based social deduction game developed by Iteration Games that uses mobile phones and tablets as controllers. Players become pirates sailing the seas, with hidden roles determining who is working together and who is secretly sabotaging the crew. Each day brings wild events that can completely change the game's dynamics.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
- Social deduction with hidden roles and traitor mechanics
- Team-based gameplay with secret objectives
- Over 60 unique daily events that transform gameplay
- Mobile device controllers for all players
- Highly customizable game settings and rules
- Events range from exploring desert islands to time travel scenarios
- Can adjust game length, event selection, ghost count, and more
- Local play or remote play via screen sharing

## Fun Factor
Paper Pirates delivers excellent social deduction fun through its event system that keeps games fresh and unpredictable. Unlike static social deduction games, the daily events inject chaos and new mechanics that force players to adapt strategies constantly. The customization options mean you can tailor experiences to your group's preferences. The pirate theme adds charm without being gimmicky. Games create genuine tension as players debate who to trust while dealing with whatever wild event the day brings. Works great for players tired of repetitive social deduction gameplay.
