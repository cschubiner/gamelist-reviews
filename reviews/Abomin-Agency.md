# Abomin-Agency!

## Game Overview
Abomin-Agency! is a party game for 3-8 players set in Dr. Frankenstein's Monster-Building Factory in the not-so-distant future. Players compete to create the best custom-tailored monsters for demanding clients, using an assortment of bizarre body parts. Each player must design monsters that meet specific client requirements to earn their paycheck and prove they're the best monster maker.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
- Design custom monsters from various body parts
- Meet specific client requirements and preferences
- 3-8 simultaneous players compete
- Phone/tablet controllers for all players
- Shared TV/screen displays the action
- Creative design challenges with scoring
- Local and online multiplayer support
- Presentation and judging phases for created monsters

## Fun Factor
Abomin-Agency! taps into creative, silly fun by letting players become monster designers. The premise is inherently funny, and watching everyone's bizarre creations come to life generates consistent laughs. The competitive element of meeting client requirements while being creative adds strategic depth to the humor. Perfect for groups who enjoy creative games like Jackbox titles. The monster-building theme is accessible and entertaining without being too niche. Judging phases where players defend their monstrous creations create memorable moments of absurd justification.
