# The Jackbox Party Pack 2

## Game Overview
The Jackbox Party Pack 2 is the second collection in the acclaimed Jackbox party game series, featuring five diverse multiplayer games that use smartphones, tablets, or web browsers as controllers. The pack offers a mix of trivia, drawing, wordplay, and improvisational games.

## Platforms
- PC (Steam)
- PlayStation 4
- PlayStation 3
- Xbox One
- Xbox 360
- Nintendo Switch
- Apple TV
- Amazon Fire TV

## Player Count
Varies by game (typically 2-8 players plus audience members)
- Players use smartphones/tablets as controllers via room codes
- Only one person needs to own the game

## Pricing
Standard Jackbox pricing (around $24.99 USD, frequent sales)

## Gameplay & Mechanics

**Included Games:**

1. **Fibbage 2** - Bluffing game where players create fake answers to fool opponents (2-8 players)
2. **Earwax** - Match sound effects to prompts (3-8 players)
3. **Bidiots** - Draw artwork and participate in art auctions (3-6 players)
4. **Quiplash XL** - Battle of wits and one-liners (3-8 players)
5. **Bomb Corp.** - Cooperative bomb defusing game (1-4 players)

Each game is accessed through a web browser on any device, with the host screen showing the main action.

## Fun Factor
Pack 2 delivers strong variety with standout games like Quiplash (often cited as one of the best Jackbox games ever) and Fibbage 2. The mix of competitive and cooperative games provides good session variety, though individual game quality varies.

## Player Count Experience
- **Optimal Range**: 4-8 players
- **Minimum**: Varies (1-3 depending on game)
- **Maximum**: 8 players (plus unlimited audience)
Quiplash and Fibbage shine with larger groups, while Bomb Corp works well with smaller teams.

## Overall Reception
Positive reception overall, with critics praising the variety and highlighting Quiplash as a standout title.

### Review Scores
- Generally positive Metacritic reviews
- User reviews mixed with an individual review citing 6.5/10, noting "Quiplash and Fibbage carried this pack"

### Notable Comments
From critic reviews:
- "A wonderfully eclectic collection of local multiplayer curiosities"
- "Somewhat uneven when it comes to quality but remains a strong choice for gamer and non-gamer folks alike"
- "Just as strong as its prequel"
- "A solid set that will provide a lot of laughs and collective fun"
- Preference between packs depends on "whether you prefer trivia, improvisation or cooperative-based games"

**Highlights:**
- Quiplash XL considered one of the best Jackbox games
- Fibbage 2 praised for accessibility and fun
- Good variety across game types

**Criticisms:**
- Uneven quality between games
- Some games don't land as well as others
- Earwax and Bomb Corp received less enthusiasm

Pack 2 is generally recommended, especially for fans of wordplay and improvisation games.
