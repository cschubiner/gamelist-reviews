# Stardust Frontier

## Game Overview
Stardust Frontier is a roguelike microgaming party adventure where two teams control giant mechs, stampede through alien planets, and face off against each other by playing micro-games on their phones. It's an innovative eight-player roguelike party game that combines strategic mech battles with quick mobile mini-games.

## Platforms

## Player Count

- Uses smartphones as controllers

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Launch the Steam game and start a lobby with the game window as your Host Monitor. All players scan a QR code on-screen to open mobile controls in their web browsers - no purchase or sign-up required. Players use their phones to defend, repair, and unleash devastating attacks against opposing teams while controlling giant mechs through alien environments.

## Fun Factor
The combination of mech battles and mobile mini-games creates a unique party game experience that emphasizes teamwork and quick reflexes. The accessibility of joining via smartphones without needing to purchase the game makes it ideal for casual gaming sessions with friends.
