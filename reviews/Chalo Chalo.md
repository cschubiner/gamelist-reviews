## Gameplay & Mechanics
Unlike traditional racing games, Chalo Chalo emphasizes strategic decision-making and tactical thinking over raw racing speed. All players compete locally on the same screen, making choices that can help them advance while potentially hindering opponents.

**Important Limitations:**
- Strictly local multiplayer only - no online options
- Requires minimum of 3 players to start
- If using Xbox One controllers, player count is capped at 4
