# Just Dance 2017

## Game Overview
Just Dance 2017 is the eighth main installment in Ubisoft's popular dance rhythm game series. Players follow on-screen dancers and match their moves to popular music tracks, with motion tracking technology scoring their performance.

## Platforms
- PlayStation 4
- PlayStation 3
- Xbox One
- Xbox 360
- Wii U
- Wii
- Nintendo Switch
- PC

## Player Count
- 1-6 players (depending on platform and available controllers/cameras)
- Solo play
- Competitive multiplayer
- Cooperative modes
- World Dance Floor (online multiplayer mode)

## Pricing
Standard retail pricing at release (around $39.99-$59.99 USD depending on platform)
Frequent sales and price reductions as newer versions release
Just Dance Unlimited subscription service for additional songs

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Players perform dance moves matching on-screen choreography while being tracked by:
- Smartphone app (using camera)
- PlayStation Camera
- Xbox Kinect
- Wii Remote
- Nintendo Switch Joy-Cons

Features:
- 40+ songs at launch
- Just Dance Unlimited subscription for 200+ additional songs
- World Dance Floor online mode
- Showtime mode (record and share performances)
- Multiple difficulty levels
- Various game modes including Solo, Duet, Trio, Quartet, and Dance Party modes

## Fun Factor
Just Dance 2017 delivers the series' signature accessible, energetic party game experience. The variety of music genres and dance styles ensures broad appeal, while the competitive and cooperative modes keep groups engaged. More enjoyable in groups than solo, as intended for the party game format.

## Player Count Experience
- **Optimal Range**: 3-6 players
- **Minimum**: 1 player
- **Maximum**: 6 players (platform dependent)
The game truly shines at parties with multiple players competing or cooperating, creating a fun, active social experience.

## Overall Reception
Generally positive reception as a solid entry in the franchise, though not considered groundbreaking.

### Review Scores
- Metacritic scores vary by platform (typically mixed to positive)
- Critics noted competent execution without major innovations

### Notable Comments
Positive aspects:
- "Another solid entry in the franchise"
- "A very good party game"
- Updated tracklist
- Fine-tuned series mechanics
- Accessible for casual players

Criticisms:
- "Has its flaws"
- "Won't satisfy those willing to play it solo"
- "Without adding great new features"
- Limited appeal for single-player focused gamers
- Incremental improvements over previous entries

General consensus: Just Dance 2017 is a competent dance party game that works best in social settings but doesn't revolutionize the series formula.
