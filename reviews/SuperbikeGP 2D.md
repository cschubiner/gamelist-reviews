# SuperbikeGP 2D

## Overview
SuperbikeGP 2D is a modern top-down 2D motorcycle racing game where players can create and customize their team, upgrade facilities to boost bike performance, and chase the world championship in campaign mode or compete against players worldwide.

## Gameplay & Mechanics

### Team and Bike Management
- Improve everything from engine power to team morale
- Rise through the ranks on your quest to become Moto 2D World Champion
- Design the perfect machine with sleek liveries and bold color schemes
- Express your style while chasing the checkered flag

### Racing Features
- Top-down arcade-style racing perspective
- Accessible gameplay with management depth
- Team upgrades and bike customization
- Campaign mode progression system

### Online and Multiplayer
- Climb online rankings in asynchronous time trials
- Multiplayer challenges against players worldwide
- Win races to unlock new bikes, skins, and gear
- Challenge riders from across the globe in high-speed time trial races
- Submit your best laps and face off against new opponents
- Ever-changing circuits and conditions

## Fun Factor
The game combines management elements with arcade-style racing in a 2D perspective, making it accessible for players while offering depth through team upgrades and bike customization. The blend of racing action and team management provides variety and replayability.

## Player Count Experience
- **Single Player**: Campaign mode with AI opponents
- **Multiplayer**: Asynchronous time trials and online challenges
- **Online Competition**: Global leaderboards and rankings
