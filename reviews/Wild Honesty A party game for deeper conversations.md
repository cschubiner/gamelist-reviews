# Wild Honesty: A party game for deeper conversations

## Game Overview
Wild Honesty is a unique party game designed to facilitate meaningful conversations by encouraging players to share opinions, thoughts, and feelings. With over 1,000 questions edited by communications expert Kalina Silverman of Big Talk, the game pushes the boundaries of friendship to deeper, more honest levels.

## Platforms
- PC (Steam)
- iOS
- Android
- Available via itch.io
- Compatible with video chat platforms via screen share

## Player Count
- 2-5 players
- Can be played remotely via video chat with screen share

## Pricing
Available on Steam, iOS App Store, Google Play, and itch.io (pricing varies by platform)

## Gameplay & Mechanics
The game alternates between:
- **Deep Conversations**: Players answer thought-provoking questions designed to facilitate meaningful sharing
- **Sound Guessing Game**: Playful intermissions to keep moods lighthearted between serious discussions

Players can customize their experience by:
- Choosing friendship level before each round (strangers to very close friends)
- Selecting from 1,000+ professionally edited questions
- Adjusting the depth and intimacy of conversations

All questions were edited by Kalina Silverman, a communications expert known for her work with Big Talk.

## Fun Factor
Unlike typical party games focused on competition or humor, Wild Honesty creates space for genuine connection and vulnerability. The balance between deep conversations and playful sound games prevents the experience from becoming too heavy while maintaining meaningful engagement.

## Player Count Experience
- **Optimal Range**: 3-5 players
- **Minimum**: 2 players
- **Maximum**: 5 players
Works well with smaller, intimate groups where participants feel comfortable being vulnerable. The lower player maximum is intentional to maintain conversation quality and ensure everyone can participate meaningfully.
