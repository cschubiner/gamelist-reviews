# Papa's Quiz

## Game Overview
Papa's Quiz is a party trivia game for 1-8 players where the smartest and fastest player will be crowned the victor. Players use phones, tablets, or controllers to compete in multiple question types with various creative twists to the standard quiz format.

## Platforms
- PC (Steam)
- Nintendo Switch
- Xbox Series X|S
- Xbox One
- PlayStation 4

## Player Count
- 1-8 players
- Up to 4 players with controllers
- Up to 8 players with mobile phones
- Uses Jackbox-style phone controllers

## Pricing
Available on Steam and console storefronts
- Frequently on sale (75% discount reported)
- Standard pricing around $9.99-$14.99 USD (varies by platform and sales)

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Papa's Quiz offers various question types and creative mechanics to differentiate itself from standard trivia games:
- Multiple quiz formats and question types
- Speed-based scoring (be smart AND fast)
- Personalization features
- Uses phones/tablets as controllers similar to Jackbox games
- Single-player, local multiplayer, and online multiplayer modes

The game attempts to bring fresh ideas to the party quiz genre with varied question formats and personality to the presentation.

## Fun Factor
The game tries different approaches to spice up the quiz game genre with creative question types and personalization options. However, the execution has mixed results with particular concerns about the voice acting quality and question balance.

## Player Count Experience
- **Optimal Range**: 4-8 players
- **Minimum**: 1 player
- **Maximum**: 8 players
Like most quiz games, it's more enjoyable with larger groups but offers solo play against AI.

## Overall Reception
Mixed reception. The game shows creativity in attempting to innovate the quiz genre but has notable flaws in execution.

### Review Scores
- Steam User Reviews: 89% Positive (115 reviews)
- Player activity: Very low (1 concurrent player, 97% below peak of 35)

### Notable Comments
**Positive aspects:**
- Tries different ways to spruce up the party game/quiz game genre
- Good job in most of its execution
- Excels in creativity to personalize the experience
- Multiple platform support

**Criticisms:**
- "Voice acting has been criticized as absolutely horrendous and completely offputting"
- "Stumbles in the quiz question balance"
- Low player activity suggests limited staying power
- Some execution issues despite creative ideas

Reviews suggest Papa's Quiz has interesting ideas and mostly solid execution, but the poor voice acting and question balance issues prevent it from reaching its full potential. The very low concurrent player count indicates the game hasn't built a sustained community.
