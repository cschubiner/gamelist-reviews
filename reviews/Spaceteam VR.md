# Spaceteam VR Review

## Overview
Spaceteam VR is a cooperative virtual reality party game based on the popular mobile game Spaceteam. Players work together as a spaceship crew, frantically coordinating to operate their vessel while shouting instructions at each other in a race against time and the dark void of space.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Up to six players must work together to survive as long as possible by following instructions, pressing buttons, pulling levers, and communicating effectively. The game creates a "hypnotic spell" where players find themselves "screaming at best friends" as they struggle to coordinate under pressure.

A unique feature is the hybrid gameplay: at least one VR headset is required, but players without headsets can join via smartphone using an official mobile app. Mobile players are represented as robots in the VR game, allowing mixed VR/non-VR groups to play together. The game also supports local play with connected smart devices.

## Fun Factor
UploadVR described the game as "quite a delight," praising how well the cooperative chaos translates to VR. The frantic nature of shouting instructions and coordinating under time pressure creates memorable, hilarious moments that exemplify what makes party games entertaining.

However, the game has received mixed reviews overall, with only 50% positive ratings on Steam, suggesting the experience may not consistently meet expectations for all players.

## Player Count Experience
- **Players:** Up to 6 players
- **VR Requirements:** At least 1 VR headset required
- **Mixed Play:** Players without VR can join via smartphone app
- **Best For:** Groups of 3-6 players with at least one VR setup

The mixed VR/mobile play option is a significant advantage, as not everyone needs expensive VR equipment to participate.

## Platforms
- **VR Platforms:**
  - Oculus Quest/Rift
  - SteamVR (HTC Vive, Valve Index, Windows Mixed Reality)
- **Cross-play:** Supported across different VR platforms
- **Mobile App:** iOS and Android for non-VR players

### Strengths
- Cooperative gameplay creates memorable moments
- Hybrid VR/mobile play is innovative and inclusive
- Cross-play support across VR platforms
- Captures the frantic fun of the original mobile game
- Up to 6 players can participate
- Mobile app allows non-VR players to join

### Weaknesses
- Mixed user reviews suggest inconsistent experience
- Limited number of reviews indicates smaller player base
- Requires at least one VR headset to play
- May be too chaotic for some players
- Replay value may be limited

## Verdict
Spaceteam VR successfully translates the mobile game's cooperative chaos into virtual reality, creating a unique party experience where communication and coordination are key. The ability to mix VR and mobile players is innovative and helps address VR's accessibility challenges. While critics found it delightful, the mixed user reviews suggest the experience may vary depending on your group's tolerance for chaotic, high-pressure gameplay. Best enjoyed with friends who appreciate shouting instructions in a panic.
