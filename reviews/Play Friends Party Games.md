# Play Friends: Party Games Review

## Overview
Play Friends: Party Games is a modern party game from Plot Interactive that emphasizes accessibility and ease of use. Supporting up to 8 players locally or remotely, it features smartphone controllers and requires no downloads for players to join - just scan a code and play.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Play Friends includes 4 mini-games designed for short, creative play sessions:
- Players use phones as controllers
- Shared-screen gameplay where everyone sees the same display
- Quick, accessible mini-games
- Works for both local couch play and remote video call sessions
- No complex setup required - players scan a code to join instantly

The game is designed around the "buy once, friends play free forever" philosophy, with no subscriptions, extra purchases, or hidden costs once the host owns the game.

## Fun Factor
The emphasis on accessibility and zero-friction entry makes Play Friends appealing for spontaneous gaming sessions. Whether gathering in person or connecting via video calls, players can jump in quickly without downloading apps or creating accounts.

The game is backed by the Google Indie Games Fund 2024, indicating industry recognition of its quality and innovation. The four mini-games provide variety while keeping the experience focused and manageable.

## Verdict
Play Friends: Party Games represents a modern, accessible approach to party gaming that addresses both traditional couch co-op and remote play scenarios. The "buy once, friends play free" model is consumer-friendly, and the zero-download entry via smartphone removes typical barriers to spontaneous gaming sessions.

While the four mini-games may seem limited compared to Jackbox's five-game packs, the focus on accessibility and the dual local/remote capability show thoughtful design. The Google Indie Games Fund 2024 backing suggests quality and innovation that industry professionals recognized.

Best suited for groups that value easy setup and want a party game that works equally well for in-person gatherings and video call hangouts. The free demo on Steam allows risk-free evaluation. If the four included games resonate with your group, the "friends play free forever" model provides excellent value. Those seeking extensive content libraries may want to wait for more games to be added or stick with established franchises, but for accessibility-focused modern party gaming, Play Friends shows promise.
