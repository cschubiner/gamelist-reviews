# Clue/Cluedo: Classic Edition

## Game Overview
Clue/Cluedo: Classic Edition is the digital adaptation of the beloved Hasbro murder mystery board game. Players work to solve the crime by deducing who committed the murder, with which weapon, and in which room of the mansion through strategic questioning and logical elimination.

## Platforms
- PC (Steam)
- Nintendo Switch
- iOS
- Android
- Cross-platform multiplayer supported

## Player Count
- 2-6 players
- Online multiplayer
- Local multiplayer
- Single-player vs AI (customizable difficulty)

## Pricing
Available on Steam (frequently on sale for 75% off)
Standard digital game pricing (around $9.99 USD base price)

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Classic Clue/Cluedo gameplay digitized:
- Players move through the mansion to different rooms
- Make accusations about suspect, weapon, and location
- Use deduction to eliminate possibilities
- First to correctly identify all three elements wins

Digital features:
- Customizable AI suspects with different difficulty levels
- Private games with friends and family
- Cross-platform multiplayer across PC, iOS, Android, and Switch
- Weekly online leaderboards
- Automated card tracking and suggestion mechanics

## Fun Factor
The digital version successfully captures the classic mystery-solving experience while removing physical board management. Cross-platform play makes it easy to organize games with friends regardless of their preferred device. The mystery-solving remains engaging, though some user experience issues can interfere with enjoyment.

## Player Count Experience
- **Optimal Range**: 4-6 players
- **Minimum**: 2 players
- **Maximum**: 6 players
More players increase the complexity of deduction as information is spread across more hands, making the mystery more challenging to solve.

## Overall Reception
Generally positive reception for bringing the classic board game to digital platforms, though noted for some user experience issues.

### Review Scores
- Reviews available on Metacritic across multiple platforms
- Steam: Mixed reviews due to UX concerns

### Notable Comments
Positive aspects:
- "A lovable take on the classic board game"
- "One of the best renditions of the murder mystery to date"
- Cross-platform multiplayer functionality praised
- Faithful adaptation of the board game
- Weekly leaderboards add competitive element

Concerns:
- "Suffering from some issues on the user-experience end"
- Some UX/UI complaints from players
- Interface could be more intuitive

Despite user experience concerns, reviewers agree it successfully delivers the core Clue experience digitally with the significant advantage of cross-platform play.
