# Party Quiz - Review Summary

Based on available information, here's a comprehensive summary of "Party Quiz," a Jeopardy-style trivia party game:

---

## Game Overview
- **Developer:** White Ink
- **Release Date:** May 31, 2023
- **Platform:** PC (Steam)
- **Price:** ~$7 (transitioned from free to paid in January 2025)
- **Status:** Early Access
- **Genre:** Trivia Party Game
- **Player Count:** Minimum 3 (1 host + 2+ players)

---

## Review Sources Found

1. **LadiesGamers** - Full written review (Rating: 3/5 "I Like it")
2. **Steam User Reviews** - 86% positive (119 reviews, "Very Positive" rating)
3. **Metacritic** - No professional critic reviews available yet (Early Access status)

---

## Gameplay & Mechanics

**Format:**
- Jeopardy-style trivia game where one player acts as the Host and others compete as Players
- Three rounds with five categories containing five questions each
- Players use phones or web browsers to answer; host runs the game from PC

**Core Mechanics:**
- Players select categories and point values, then race to "ring in" with typed answers
- Host manually validates correct answers (no automatic checking system)
- Strategic element: question-choosers can assign difficult categories to opponents
- Supports custom question creation via Party Quiz Constructor
- Steam Workshop integration for community-created quiz packs with difficulty filters

**Technical Implementation:**
- Colorful, blocky, Minecraft-inspired aesthetic
- Web-based interface for players (no controller needed)
- Supports both local and online multiplayer

---

## Fun Factor

**Positives:**
- LadiesGamers: "Very well put together... overall a lot of fun"
- Wide range of knowledge topics (video games, authors, mushrooms, etc.) keeps gameplay diverse and unpredictable
- Custom question creation allows personalized gameplay for friend groups
- Successfully facilitates social entertainment at gatherings

**Quirks:**
- Manual answer verification creates an "awkward" interface
- However, this design choice solves common frustrations with strict answer-matching in other trivia games (spelling disputes)

---

## Player Count Experience

**Strengths:**
- Designed specifically for party groups with extended playtime
- Scales well for larger groups
- Community feedback shows successful use at get-togethers and social events

**Requirements:**
- Needs minimum 3 players (1 host + 2+ players)
- Best suited for groups willing to commit to extended sessions

**Limitations:**
- Some users reported server connectivity issues preventing simultaneous room joining
- Technical problems with website loading in certain regions

---

## Overall Reception

**Critical Reception:**
- **LadiesGamers**: 3/5 rating - Recommended for trivia enthusiasts despite quirks
- **No professional gaming press reviews** - Likely due to Early Access status and pricing transition

**User Reception:**
- **Steam**: 86% positive (Very Positive rating) from 119 reviews
- Players noted: "It's playable, and it's obvious what the goals are and what's going on"
- Host, matchmaker, and web systems work "with no major bugs"

**Developer Support:**
- Active post-launch support fixing spelling/grammar errors
- Regular updates adding features (chat, custom avatars, new media formats)
- Shows commitment to quality improvement

**Caveats:**
- Game is in **Early Access** (not complete)
- Transitioned from free to paid (~$7) in January 2025
- Some technical issues remain (AI host features, player info display)

---

## Key Takeaway
Party Quiz receives generally positive reception as a solid trivia party game that successfully replicates the Jeopardy experience for home gatherings. Critics appreciate its customization options and social gameplay, though the manual answer verification system and Early Access status mean it still has rough edges.
