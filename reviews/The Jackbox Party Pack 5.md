# The Jackbox Party Pack 5

## Game Overview
The Jackbox Party Pack 5 is the fifth installment in the popular Jackbox party game series, featuring five distinct party games including the return of the beloved You Don't Know Jack trivia franchise, along with new creative offerings like Mad Verse City, Patently Stupid, Zeeple Dome, and Split the Room.

## Platforms
- PC (Steam)
- PlayStation 4
- PlayStation 5
- Xbox One
- Xbox Series X|S
- Nintendo Switch
- Available on multiple other platforms

## Player Count
Varies by game (typically 3-8 players plus audience members)
- Players use smartphones, tablets, or computers as controllers
- Only host needs to own the game

## Pricing
Standard Jackbox pricing (around $29.99 USD, varies by platform and sales)

## Gameplay & Mechanics

**Included Games:**

1. **You Don't Know Jack: Full Stream** - The classic trivia game returns with modern twists
2. **Mad Verse City** - Create and perform rap battles
3. **Patently Stupid** - Draw and pitch ridiculous inventions
4. **Zeeple Dome** - An experimental action game where players fling aliens
5. **Split the Room** - Create hypothetical scenarios to divide your friends

Each game uses smartphones/devices as controllers via room codes, making it accessible for groups without needing multiple physical controllers.

## Fun Factor
Pack 5 delivers strong variety with the triumphant return of You Don't Know Jack being a major highlight. Mad Verse City's rap battles generate consistent laughs, and Patently Stupid cleverly combines drawing with presentation skills. Even the weakest entry, Split the Room, has its charms.

## Player Count Experience
- **Optimal Range**: 4-8 players
- **Minimum**: Varies by game (typically 2-3)
- **Maximum**: 8 players (plus unlimited audience)
The Audience feature allows additional participants to influence games even when player slots are full.

## Overall Reception
Very positive reception. Critics praised the variety and quality of games included, with particular acclaim for You Don't Know Jack's return.

### Review Scores
- Metacritic User Score: 7.4/10
- 63% Positive reviews
- 31% Mixed reviews

### Notable Comments
From critic reviews:
- "Jackbox Party Pack 5 delivers on another great set of party games"
- "You Don't Know Jack returns better than ever"
- "Mad Verse City has the potential to create plenty of laughs"
- "Zeeple Dome is a successful experiment"
- "Patently Stupid mixes drawings and prompts in the most clever way yet"
- "Split the Room" is "the least exciting of the bunch" but still has "its own charms"

Pack 5 is considered one of the stronger entries in the series, with most games landing well with party game audiences.
