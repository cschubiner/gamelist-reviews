# DuckHunt - Missouri

## Game Information
- **Developer:** Mobile TV Games
- **Publisher:** Mobile TV Games
- **Platforms:** Steam (Windows, Mac), Android (Google Play), iOS (App Store)
- **Release Date:** March 21, 2022
- **Price:** $1.99 (Steam); Free apps (Android/iOS)
- **Player Count:** Literally unlimited (any number of smartphones can connect)
- **Unique Feature:** Uses smartphone cameras as controllers via patented computer vision technology

## Overview
DuckHunt - Missouri is a multiplayer duck hunting game that uses novel patented computer vision technology to enable players to shoot directly on the screen using their cell phones. It's similar to the original classic DuckHunt game, but now in multiplayer mode with literally no limit on the number of players who can participate simultaneously.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Using novel patented technology, players use their cellphones to aim at the screen and shoot. The system is super simple to use with absolutely no calibration required - you just connect your smartphone via the free mobile app and start shooting. The cellphone camera establishes where you are aiming at the computer screen or TV automatically.

The game maintains the classic duck hunting gameplay loop: ducks fly across the screen, and players try to shoot them before they escape. The multiplayer aspect adds competitive elements as multiple hunters compete for the highest score. Ultra-simple connection through free apps for both iOS and Android makes setup quick and painless.

The main game runs on PC or Mac, while each player uses their smartphone as a wireless light gun controller through the companion app.

## Fun Factor
DuckHunt - Missouri successfully modernizes the classic arcade shooting gallery experience for contemporary multiplayer gaming. The novelty of using your smartphone as a gun controller adds appeal, and the no-calibration setup means you spend more time playing than configuring. The unlimited player support makes it ideal for parties where everyone can participate simultaneously.

The familiar gameplay is immediately understandable to anyone who remembers classic duck hunting games, while the smartphone technology feels fresh and modern.

## Player Count Experience
The unlimited player support is the game's standout feature. Whether you have 2 players or 20, everyone can play at the same time rather than taking turns. This makes it perfect for parties, bars, gatherings, or events where you want large group participation. The free mobile apps mean there's no per-player cost beyond the initial $1.99 game purchase.
