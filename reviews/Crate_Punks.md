# Crate Punks - Review Summary

Based on my research, I found limited critical reviews from major gaming outlets for "Crate Punks," a small indie game released in 2016. However, I was able to gather information from available sources including the Steam store page, itch.io page, and community feedback. Here's a structured summary:

---

## CRATE PUNKS - REVIEW SUMMARY

### **Game Overview**
- **Developer:** Laborious Rex (indie studio)
- **Release Date:** December 2, 2016
- **Price:** $3.00 USD
- **Platform:** PC (Steam, itch.io)

---

### **GAMEPLAY & MECHANICS**

**Core Concept:**
- Arena brawler where every arena is constructed entirely of crates
- Central mechanic: "Anything you can stand on can be used as a weapon"
- Described as "chaotic couch play" with "crate action chess" elements
- Last-player-standing elimination format with no respawning

**Gameplay Structure:**
- Short, violent bursts of crate-thrashing combat
- Environmental destruction is integral to strategy
- Replay system shows final moments before the last player is eliminated
- Point-based competitive matches

---

### **FUN FACTOR**

**Positive Reception:**
- itch.io rating: **4.7 out of 5 stars** (based on 3 ratings)
- Described as offering "the greatest crate-throwing experience since the boston tea party"
- Features what developers call "the juiciest crates in years"
- Chaotic and fast-paced gameplay suits party game atmosphere

**Limited Feedback:**
- Steam shows only **6 user reviews** (5 positive, 1 negative) - insufficient for official score
- Very limited player base and review coverage
- 6 Steam curators have reviewed it, but specific feedback not widely available

---

### **PLAYER COUNT EXPERIENCE**

**Multiplayer Focus:**
- **Local multiplayer only** (couch co-op/versus)
- Supports **1-16 players** (itch.io listing)
- **Minimum requirement:** At least 2 players (1 human friend required)
- No single-player or online modes

**Technical Support:**
- Shared/split screen PvP
- USB controller support (recommended over keyboard)
- Mixed input support (keyboard + controllers simultaneously)
- Remote Play Together capability via Steam
- Up to 2 keyboard players can play simultaneously

### **KEY TAKEAWAYS**

**Strengths:**
- Unique destructible environment mechanic
- Affordable price point ($3)
- Supports large local multiplayer sessions (up to 16 players)
- Fast-paced party game design

**Limitations:**
- Requires multiple players (not suitable for solo play)
- No online multiplayer
- Extremely limited critical coverage
- Small player community
- Early technical issues (since addressed)

**Best For:** Groups looking for an inexpensive, chaotic local multiplayer party game with unique environmental destruction mechanics.
