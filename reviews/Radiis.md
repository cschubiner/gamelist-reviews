# Radiis

## Overview
Radiis is a turn-based strategy game with a unique twist: there are no moving units, only buildings. Players place buildings and capture nearby tiles to grow their population, then use those buildings to destroy enemy buildings and claim their territory.

## Gameplay & Mechanics

### Core Gameplay
- Turn-based strategy without moving units
- Building placement is the primary mechanic
- Capture nearby tiles to expand territory
- Use buildings to attack enemy structures

### Strategic Elements
- Population growth through territorial expansion
- Building synergies and placement strategies
- Resource management through territory control
- Tactical building destruction and defense

### Game Modes
- **Campaign Mode**: Over 20 hours of handcrafted missions
- **Skirmish Mode**: Randomly generated maps for variety
- **Multiplayer**: Up to 16 players per game

### Player Options
- AI opponents
- Local players
- Remote Play Together support

## Fun Factor
Radiis offers a refreshing take on turn-based strategy by removing unit movement entirely and focusing on building placement. This unique concept creates a different kind of strategic depth that appeals to players looking for something new in the genre. The minimalist design philosophy keeps the focus on strategic decisions without overwhelming complexity.

## Player Count Experience
