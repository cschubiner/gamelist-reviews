# Republic of Jungle

## Game Information
- **Developer:** Gerdoo Games
- **Publisher:** Gerdoo Games
- **Platforms:** Windows, Mac, Linux, Steam (with console and Smart TV versions in development)
- **Release Date:** February 8, 2024 (out of Early Access)
- **Price:** Free to Play
- **Player Count:** 5-10 players

## Overview
Republic of Jungle is an online social deduction game where 5 to 10 players role-play as advisors to President Puma. His Loyalists try to form a taskforce to deal with scandals as they happen, but a few Leakers among them are trying to sneak onto the taskforce to leak the scandals to Fiona Fox, a prominent news anchor.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
The game features a novel social deduction experience with continuous action that creates a more immersive, intuitive and dynamic gameplay loop. Players can entertain friends at their house, play remotely with online pals, or host a DIY game show for stream audiences. The game offers unique Discord Integration, allowing players to connect the game to their Discord voice chat so friends can jump into the game with one click and play using the Discord UI.

The guided experience makes the game accessible to players of all skill levels, while the novel mechanics add a new depth of skills to master for experienced social deduction fans.

## Fun Factor
Republic of Jungle provides an engaging social deduction experience with its unique theme and continuous action mechanics. The Discord integration makes it particularly appealing for online gaming communities and streamers looking for interactive content with their audiences.

## Player Count Experience
Designed specifically for 5-10 players, the game offers flexibility in group sizes. The social deduction mechanics work best with at least 5 players to create enough uncertainty and intrigue, while the maximum of 10 players keeps the game manageable and prevents it from becoming too chaotic.
