## Gameplay & Mechanics

**Core Gameplay:**
- Players draw prompts while others guess what's being drawn (similar to Pictionary)
- Supports both local couch co-op and online multiplayer
- Players use smartphones as controllers alongside a big screen device (PC, Mac, Linux, or smart TV)

**Hybrid Play System:**
- Supports crossplay with seamless mixing of local and online players in the same session
- Device Setup: Requires a big screen (computer/TV) plus smartphones for each player (no streaming required)

**AI Integration:**
- Features optional image recognition AI that analyzes drawings and provides its own guesses
- Players can select AI guesses as alternatives

**Content Creation:**
- Players can submit their own phrases in multiple languages (27+ languages supported)
