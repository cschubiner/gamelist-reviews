# Click on clocks together

## Overview
Click on clocks together is a casual hidden object game where players search an incredibly detailed hand-drawn environment to find all 100+ hidden clocks. The game emphasizes relaxed, cooperative gameplay with no time limits, supporting various input methods including local multiplayer, online play, and LAN connections.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Click on clocks together delivers a straightforward hidden object experience centered on finding clocks within a panoramic hand-drawn scene.

**Core Gameplay:**
- **Search Mechanism:** Click and drag with mouse to explore the detailed panoramic image
- **Object Marking:** Click on clocks to mark them as found
- **Completion:** Game concludes when all 100+ clocks are discovered
- **Scoreboard:** Final screen shows which player found which items

**Difficulty:** Rated as Medium

The game's hand-drawn art style creates visual complexity that makes finding all clocks genuinely challenging without being frustrating. The medium difficulty suggests a balance between casual accessibility and satisfying discovery.

## Fun Factor
Click on clocks together provides relaxing cooperative gameplay perfect for casual sessions. The absence of time limits removes pressure, allowing players to search at their own pace and discuss potential clock locations.

The detailed hand-drawn artwork makes exploration enjoyable beyond just finding clocks - players can appreciate the artistic effort and visual details. The collaborative aspect encourages communication and shared discovery rather than competition.

The 100+ clocks provide substantial content for the scope, ensuring sessions last long enough to feel worthwhile without overstaying their welcome.

## Player Count Experience
**Supported Players:** 1+ players (single-player and multiplayer)

**Control Options:**
- **Mouse:** Default control method
- **Keyboard:** WASD, TFGH, IJKL, or arrow keys
- **Gamepads:** Controller support
- **Steam Remote Play:** Play remotely with friends
- **LAN:** Local network play
- **Online:** Internet multiplayer (no port forwarding needed, connectionless)

The extensive control and connectivity options make the game remarkably accessible for various group configurations. Players can:
- Gather locally with multiple controllers
- Play online with distant friends
- Use Steam Remote Play for easy online sessions
- Connect over LAN for local network parties

The scoreboard tracking who found what adds a gentle competitive element to the cooperative searching, creating mild bragging rights without serious competition pressure.
