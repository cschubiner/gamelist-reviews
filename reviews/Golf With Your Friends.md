# Golf With Your Friends

## Overview
Golf With Your Friends is a fast-paced, exciting, simultaneous mini golf game for up to 12 players. Described as "the perfect game to turn your brain off to while goofing off with friends on Discord," it offers an extreme mini golf experience with creative courses and multiplayer chaos.

## Gameplay & Mechanics

### Core Gameplay
- Simultaneous mini golf (all players play at once)
- Up to 12 players per game
- Fast-paced and accessible
- Multiple game modes

### Course Content
- 7 themed courses in base game
- 18 holes per course
- Creative and challenging hole designs
- Variety of themes and environments

### Multiplayer Features
- Host your own servers
- Join other players through server browser
- Simultaneous play keeps games moving quickly
- Perfect for casual Discord sessions

### Workshop Support
- Players can create their own maps and courses
- Play courses designed by community members
- Extensive customization options
- Active modding community

## Fun Factor
Golf With Your Friends excels as a casual multiplayer experience. The simultaneous play keeps everyone engaged without waiting for turns, and the creative course designs provide plenty of variety. It's perfect for relaxed social gaming sessions while chatting with friends. The workshop support adds nearly unlimited replayability through community-created content.
