# Bingo Hall - Review Summary

Based on available information, here's a comprehensive summary of "Bingo Hall," a PC indie digital bingo party game:

---

## Game Overview
- **Developer:** John Vieira
- **Publisher:** Angry Otter Games
- **Release Date:** January 21, 2020
- **Platform:** PC (Steam) - Currently Delisted
- **Genre:** Casual Party Game / Bingo
- **Player Count:** 2-50+ players

---

## Review Sources Found
1. **Steam Community Reviews** - User reviews (78% positive from 28 reviews)
2. **Metacritic** - Limited coverage
3. **SteamSpy** - Statistical data

---

## Gameplay & Mechanics

**What Users Say:**
- **Automated System**: The game automatically marks numbers when called, which received mixed reactions. One reviewer criticized this, stating "a number gets called and it automatically dabs it for you, you are literally just sitting there waiting"
- **Management Tool**: Described positively as "a one-click random number generator that made it easy to host games with friends"
- **Control Options**: Offers both manual controls and automated experience with announcer
- **Pattern Variety**: Includes standard bingo patterns - 1 Line, 2 Lines, 4 Corners, T, X, and Full Card

---

## Fun Factor

**What Users Say:**
- **Enthusiast Appeal**: Players with bingo-loving family members reported success - one user noted their "wife and mother-in-law are bingo fanatics" and "loved it"
- **Casual Player Reception**: More casual players found it underwhelming, citing lack of customization options
- **Simplicity**: Consistently described as "a very fun and simple game to play"
- **Value Proposition**: Most reviewers acknowledged the low price point as justifying the basic feature set

---

## Player Count Experience

**What Users Say:**
- **Local Mode**: Supports "almost any number of people" for in-person parties
- **Online Mode**: Hosts up to 50 players via browser-based system with room codes
- **Accessibility**: Players can join using real bingo cards or mobile phones
- **UI Issues**: Some users reported difficulty with online features

---

## Overall Reception

**Ratings:**
- **Steam**: 78% positive reviews (from 28 total reviews)
- **Current Status**: Game has been delisted from Steam

**Key Takeaways:**
- Best suited for hosting casual family/friend gatherings for dedicated bingo enthusiasts
- Niche appeal with strongest reception from bingo enthusiasts
- Mixed feelings about passive gameplay with automatic marking
- Limited feature set acceptable given pricing
