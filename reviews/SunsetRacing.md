# Sunset Racing

## Game Overview
Sunset Racing is a casual top-down RC racing party game inspired by classic karting games and nostalgic coffee table arcade cabinets. The game features a Couch Party Mode designed for up to 4 players using controllers, delivering arcade-style racing action that can be enjoyed on devices like Steam Deck or scaled up for epic group races on larger screens.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
- Top-down RC racing perspective
- Classic karting game mechanics
- Couch Party Mode with local multiplayer
- Supports up to 4 players simultaneously
- 4 controllers required for full multiplayer
- Arcade-inspired physics and handling
- Multiple tracks and racing modes
- Casual, accessible racing gameplay
- Steam Deck compatible

## Fun Factor
Sunset Racing captures the pick-up-and-play charm of classic arcade racers. The top-down perspective makes it easy for players of all skill levels to compete on equal footing. The game excels at creating quick, exciting races perfect for party situations where you want fast rounds and immediate action. The RC racing theme adds a unique flavor to the karting formula. Best for groups seeking straightforward, unpretentious racing fun without complex mechanics or steep learning curves. The nostalgic arcade cabinet inspiration gives it retro appeal.
