# Click on cups together

## Overview
Click on cups together is a casual hidden object game where players search an incredibly detailed hand-drawn panoramic environment to find all 100+ hidden cups. Like its sibling game "Click on clocks together," it emphasizes relaxed, cooperative gameplay with no time limits and supports multiple input methods including local multiplayer, online play, and LAN connections.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Click on cups together delivers a focused hidden object experience centered on finding cups within a hand-drawn panoramic scene.

**Core Gameplay:**
- **Search Mechanism:** Click and drag with mouse to navigate the detailed panoramic image
- **Object Marking:** Click on cups to mark them as found
- **Completion:** Game concludes when all 100+ cups are discovered
- **Scoreboard:** Final screen displays which player found which items

**Difficulty:** Rated as Hard

The "Hard" difficulty rating suggests this entry in the series presents a more challenging search than other variants. The cups may be more cleverly hidden, smaller, or better integrated into the artwork, requiring more careful observation and patience.

## Fun Factor
Click on cups together provides relaxing cooperative gameplay perfect for casual gaming sessions. The absence of time limits creates a stress-free environment where players can search methodically and discuss potential cup locations without pressure.

The hand-drawn panoramic artwork makes exploration visually engaging beyond the mechanical search for cups. The collaborative nature encourages communication and shared discovery moments when someone spots a particularly well-hidden cup.

With 100+ cups to find and Hard difficulty rating, the game provides substantial challenge for hidden object enthusiasts while remaining accessible to newcomers through unlimited hints and the no-pressure format.

## Player Count Experience
**Supported Players:** 1+ players (single-player and multiplayer)

**Control Options:**
- **Mouse:** Default control method
- **Keyboard:** WASD, TFGH, IJKL, or arrow keys for navigation
- **Gamepads:** Full controller support
- **Steam Remote Play:** Play remotely with friends

The extensive connectivity options make the game highly accessible for various group setups:
- Local gatherings with multiple controllers

The scoreboard tracking creates mild competitive elements within the cooperative framework, giving players bragging rights for their discoveries without serious competitive pressure.
