# Carrot Commotion

## Game Overview
Carrot Commotion is a social deduction game show where up to eight players compete over four fast-paced rounds to win the Carrot Cup. Players must keep the contents of their box secret while deducing what's in other players' boxes, combining strategy with social deduction mechanics.

## Platforms
- PC (Steam)

## Player Count
- Up to 8 players
- 8 unique characters to choose from
- Uses web-enabled devices (smartphones, tablets)

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Players choose from 8 unique characters and compete in a game show format where:
- Each player receives a box with secret contents
- Players must deduce what's in other players' boxes
- Social deduction and strategy are key across four rounds
- Web-enabled devices serve as controllers

The game does NOT support online matchmaking but CAN be enjoyed remotely using:
- Live streaming services
- Video conferencing tools

## Fun Factor
