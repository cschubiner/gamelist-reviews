# The Jackbox Party Pack 11

## Game Overview
The Jackbox Party Pack 11 is the latest installment in the beloved party game series, featuring five all-new games that showcase continued creativity and understanding of group dynamics. Released in October 2025, it brings fresh takes on trivia, audio challenges, and social gameplay.

## Platforms

- PlayStation 5

- Nintendo Switch

## Gameplay & Mechanics

**Included Games (based on available information):**

1. **Legends of Trivia** - Collaborative fantasy-themed trivia game
2. **Hear Say** - Absurd audio-based party game
3. Three additional new games (specific titles not detailed in search results)

As with all Jackbox games, players join via room codes on their personal devices, with the main screen showing the action and results.

## Fun Factor
Pack 11 "leans into originality and variety" with new game concepts that go beyond simple retreads of earlier titles. The mix of collaborative trivia and audio-based challenges provides different types of party game experiences for varied group preferences.

## Overall Reception
Positive but mixed reception compared to earlier acclaimed packs. Critics appreciate the creativity but some feel it doesn't reach the heights of earlier series entries.

### Review Scores
- **Metacritic (PC)**: 81/100
  - Impulsegamer: 92
  - Noisy Pixel: 85
  - CGMagazine: 85
  - TheSixthAxis: 80
  - Loot Level Chill: 75

### Notable Comments
**Positive:**
- "A lively mix of five all-new party games"
- "Showcasing Jackbox Games' continued creativity and understanding of group dynamics"
- "Leans into originality and variety"
- Legends of Trivia praised for collaborative fantasy trivia concept
- Hear Say noted for "absurd audio fun"

**Criticisms:**
- "Feels like one of the weaker releases when compared to earlier games in the series"
- Some reviewers felt it didn't reach the quality peaks of packs like 3 or 5

**Overall:** Pack 11 demonstrates Jackbox's continued innovation and quality, earning an 81 Metascore, though it may not be the absolute strongest entry in the long-running series. Still recommended for Jackbox fans and party game enthusiasts.
