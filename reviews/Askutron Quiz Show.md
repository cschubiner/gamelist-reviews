# Askutron Quiz Show

## Game Overview
Askutron Quiz Show is a trivia game for up to 8 players that features fully voiced questions for both official and user-generated content. It offers multiple ways to play including local multiplayer with various controller options and experimental online multiplayer.

## Platforms
- PC (Steam)
- Mobile devices (iOS, Android) via Askutron Buzzer app

## Player Count
- Up to 8 players
- Supports keyboard/mouse, gamepads, and mobile devices
- Asynchronous play available through "Quizatars"

## Pricing
Available on Steam (specific price not listed in search results)

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Players compete in trivia challenges with fully voiced questions. The game supports multiple input methods: up to 8 gamepads, keyboard/mouse, or mobile devices via QR code scanning. A unique feature is the "Quizatars" system, which remembers every answer given and allows you to play against AI representations of your friends that answer exactly as they would.

The game includes:
- Local Multiplayer: Up to 8 gamepads and/or mobile devices
- Online Multiplayer: Cross-platform but still experimental
- Asynchronous Play: Compete against "Quizatars" of your friends

## Fun Factor
The fully voiced questions and variety of input methods make this accessible and entertaining for groups. The user-generated question feature adds replayability, though the experimental nature of online multiplayer may limit some experiences.

## Player Count Experience
- **Optimal Range**: 4-8 players
- **Minimum**: 1 player (can play against Quizatars)
- **Maximum**: 8 players
Works well with larger groups due to flexible controller options and the competitive trivia format.
