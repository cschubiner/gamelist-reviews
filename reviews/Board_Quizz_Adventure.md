# Board Quizz Adventure - Review Summary

**Important Notice:** Board Quizz Adventure is a small indie party game released in May 2019 with **very limited professional review coverage**. Steam shows only 4 total reviews with insufficient data to generate an overall score.

---

## Available Critical Feedback

### Gameplay & Mechanics

**Core Concept:**
- Blends quiz mechanics with board game progression beyond traditional buzzer-style trivia games
- Players answer questions to clear paths on a board and race to reach the village objective first
- Wrong answers permanently block paths, forcing teams to find alternative routes
- 40 unique maps with varying layouts and strategic considerations

**Team Structure:**
- Supports 1v1, 2v2, 4v4, or asymmetric teams (2v3) as long as teams are balanced
- One player per team controls movement with a controller/keyboard
- Other team members collaborate on answering questions and determining optimal paths

**Question Categories:**
- 3,000 questions across 23 categories including Sports, History, Science & Nature, Video Games, Anime & Manga, and General Knowledge

---

### Fun Factor

**Positive Reception:**
- Described by its creators as "perfect to play while having a drink with friends"
- Community members found it "cute and original"
- Praised for being accessible to non-gamers, as not everyone needs to hold a controller
- The blend of trivia with strategic path-finding was appreciated as innovative

**Concerns:**
- Limited player base suggests the novelty may not have sustained long-term engagement
- Lack of single-player AI opponents reduces replayability for solo sessions
- Some players found the interface unclear, noting hotkey information wasn't visible outside tutorials

---

### Player Count Experience

**Multiplayer Support:**
- Local multiplayer ONLY (no online play)
- Supports unlimited players with just 2 controllers
- Designed as a team-based party game

**Community Concerns:**
- Players questioned whether the game works well with more than 2 teams
- Lack of online multiplayer significantly limits accessibility
- No computer opponents means solo play isn't viable

**Suggested Improvements:**
- Community requested boss encounters every 5 levels requiring cooperative time-limited challenges
- Players wanted clarification on whether the game targets adults or children

## Summary

Board Quizz Adventure is a niche indie party game that attempted to innovate on traditional trivia formats by adding board game strategy elements. While the core concept received praise for being "cute and original" with accessible team-based gameplay, the game suffers from extremely limited coverage and player adoption. The local-only multiplayer, lack of single-player options, and minimal content updates appear to have prevented the game from building a sustainable audience.
