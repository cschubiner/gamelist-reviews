# Awkward 2: The Party Game of Savage Secrets

## Game Overview
Awkward 2 is an adult multiplayer party game developed by Snap Finger Click, filled with dark, hilarious, and sometimes downright disturbing questions. Players laugh, debate, defend their answers, and discover things they never wanted to know about their friends through over 10,000 savage questions designed to create uncomfortable and entertaining situations.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
- Question-based party game with provocative and controversial prompts
- Players answer questions and defend their choices
- Debate and discussion mechanics encourage group interaction
- Over 10,000 questions ensure variety and replayability
- Phone controllers for all players
- Supports both small groups and massive multiplayer gatherings
- Adult-oriented content with mature themes

## Fun Factor
Awkward 2 lives up to its name by creating genuinely awkward, hilarious situations that reveal surprising things about your friends. The massive question database keeps gameplay fresh across multiple sessions. The game shines when played with friends who have thick skin and enjoy dark humor. Debates can get heated (in a fun way) as players justify their choices. The "savage secrets" nature means you'll learn things about friends you can't unlearn. Best for groups that enjoy Cards Against Humanity-style humor and aren't easily offended.

## Player Count Experience
- **Supported Players**: 2-8 players standard
