# PICO PARK

## Game Overview
PICO PARK is a cooperative multiplayer action-puzzle independent game developed by Japanese developer TECOPARK. The game challenges players to work together to solve puzzles and complete levels, requiring tight coordination and communication. Players must cooperate or face collective failure in this charming, minimalist puzzle platformer.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
- Cooperative puzzle-platformer requiring teamwork and coordination
- Three distinct game modes:
  - **World Mode**: Progress through puzzle levels by completing objectives
  - **Endless Mode**: Compete for high scores in endless challenges
  - **Battle Mode**: Competitive mode where players compete instead of cooperate
- 2-8 players must work together simultaneously
- Simple controls but complex cooperation requirements
- Levels scale in difficulty based on player count
- Minimalist visual style with cute animal characters

## Fun Factor
PICO PARK delivers exceptional cooperative fun that tests friendships and communication skills. The game's genius lies in creating situations where every player matters - one person's mistake affects everyone. This leads to hilarious failures and immensely satisfying successes. The Battle Mode provides a competitive outlet when cooperation becomes too intense. The simple mechanics hide deep coordination challenges that keep groups engaged. Perfect for creating memorable gaming moments with friends, though be warned: friendships may be tested!

## Player Count Experience
- **Supported Players**: 2-8 players online or local
- **Optimal Experience**: 4-6 players offers the best balance of chaos and coordination
- **Minimum Players**: Requires at least 2 players (not a single-player experience)
- **Scalable Difficulty**: Puzzles automatically adjust based on player count
- **Online Multiplayer**: Full online support for remote play
