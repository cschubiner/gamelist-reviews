# Clue/Cluedo Review

## Overview
Clue (known as Cluedo in most countries outside North America) is the classic murder mystery board game that has been adapted into digital formats across multiple platforms. Players take on the role of detectives trying to solve a murder by determining who committed the crime, where it occurred, and with what weapon.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
Clue/Cluedo is a deduction-based game where 2-6 players move around a mansion, gathering clues to solve a murder mystery. Players use process of elimination to determine the culprit, location, and weapon from a pool of possibilities. The digital versions maintain the classic gameplay while adding quality-of-life improvements like automated note-taking and streamlined turn processing.

The game supports cross-play between different versions, allowing players on various platforms to compete together. The presentation and execution are praised for being faithful to the original while modernizing the experience for digital play.

## Fun Factor
The game's enduring popularity stems from its engaging deduction mechanics and the satisfaction of piecing together clues to solve the mystery. The digital versions offer great presentation and smooth execution, making the game accessible to new players while retaining the strategic depth that longtime fans appreciate.

However, some versions lack local multiplayer options, requiring online play for multi-player sessions, which may disappoint those looking for couch co-op experiences.

## Player Count Experience
- **Physical Board Game:** 2-6 players (varies by edition, with 3-6 being most common)
- **Digital Versions:** 2-6 players with both online and single-player modes

The game works best with 3-6 players, providing enough suspects and interaction to keep the deduction interesting without making games overly long.

## Overall Reception

### Critical Scores
- **Steam Classic Edition:** 87% positive (2,058 reviews)
- **Steam (2024 version):** 91% positive (381 reviews)
- **Switch version:** Mixed reception - praised for presentation but criticized for lack of local multiplayer

### Strengths
- Faithful digital adaptation of the classic game
- Great presentation and execution
- Cross-play support
- Accessible to all ages
- Strong deduction gameplay

### Weaknesses
- Some versions lack single-system multiplayer
- Limited innovation compared to the original board game
- Mobile version may have fewer features than console versions

## Verdict
Clue/Cluedo successfully translates the classic murder mystery board game to digital platforms while maintaining the core deduction gameplay that has made it a household favorite for decades. The digital versions offer convenience and polish, though players seeking local couch co-op should check platform-specific features before purchasing.
