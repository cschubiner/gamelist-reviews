# Use Your Words 2

## Overview
Use Your Words 2 is described as "the party game for funny people and their unfunny friends." This sequel features all-new prompts and twice as many mini-games as the original, where players compete to write the funniest punchlines. It builds on the foundation of the first game with enhanced features, more content, and improved streaming support.

## Gameplay & Mechanics

### Core Gameplay
- Write funny punchlines for various scenarios
- Compete with friends for the best responses
- Vote on each other's submissions
- Score points for getting votes
- Multiple mini-games with different formats

### Eight Unique Mini-Games
**Sub The Title:**
- Write subtitles for weird foreign film clips
- Make actors say what you want
- Creative storytelling with video context

**Book It!:**
- Write titles for blank book covers
- Create funny or clever titles
- Visual prompt system

**Blank-o-Matic:**
- Fill in the blank to complete sentences
- Matches your sense of humor
- Classic party game format
- Flexible responses

**Plus 5 More Mini-Games:**
- Twice as many as original game
- Variety keeps gameplay fresh
- Different challenge types

### Custom Playlist System
- Create your own custom playlist of mini-games
- Play games in any order you choose
- Can play favorite mini-game multiple times in a row
- Tailored experience for your group

### Controller System
- Use phones or tablets as controllers
- No additional hardware needed
- Easy setup and joining
- Seamless gameplay experience

### Spectator Mode
- Up to 1,000 spectators supported
- Join interactive meta-game
- Change main players' scores
- Trivia questions for spectators
- Local or remote spectators
- Keeps eliminated/waiting players engaged

### Streamer Mode
- Optimized for content creation
- Royalty-free music included
- Extended timers for streaming
- Viewers can play along without buying
- Free participation for audience

## Fun Factor
Use Your Words 2 excels at generating laughter through creative writing and voting. The variety of eight mini-games keeps sessions fresh, and the ability to customize playlists means you can focus on your group's favorites. The spectator system is particularly clever, allowing huge groups to participate. Perfect for parties, streaming, or casual online hangouts. The humor comes from the players, so it's best with creative, funny friends.
