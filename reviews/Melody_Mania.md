# Melody Mania - Review Summary

Based on available information, here's a comprehensive summary of "Melody Mania," a karaoke party game:

---

## Game Overview
- **Developer:** Andreas Achim Stange
- **Release Date:** July 3, 2023 (Early Access)
- **Platforms:** Windows, Mac, Linux, Steam Deck
- **Price:** $18.99 (frequently on sale for 15% off)
- **Genre:** Karaoke/Rhythm/Music Simulation
- **Player Count:** 1-2 local co-op, experimental online multiplayer

---

## Overall Reception

**Rating:** Mostly Positive (78-79% positive)
- **Steam Reviews:** 78% positive from 60 reviews
- **Player Score:** 79-83/100
- **Breakdown:** 49 positive vs. 13 negative reviews on Steam
- **Current Status:** Early Access (released July 3, 2023)

The game has received solid approval from players, though as an Early Access title, it's still being developed and refined.

---

## Gameplay & Mechanics

**Core Mechanics:**
- Pitch detection AI that guides vocal performance
- Support for custom songs in UltraStar or MIDI format
- Integrated song editor with AI tools to create sing-along versions
- AI-powered vocal isolation for classic karaoke experience
- Video support from YouTube and local files

**Technical Feedback:**
- **Positive:** Players appreciate the open song format and "boundless possibilities" offered by the editor tools
- **Negative:** Setup can be challenging - users report difficulty syncing microphone, speakers, and screen. The AI tools require "a lot of tinkering to get songs imported into the game in a playable state"
- **Microphone Issues:** Some users encountered limitations with multiple microphone support

---

## Fun Factor

**Positive Reception:**
- Described as "a fun party game" with family-friendly content
- One reviewer enthusiastically stated: "This revolution of karaoke never gets old"
- Flexibility in play styles (competitive, cooperative, or casual without ratings) enhances enjoyment
- Queue and medley features allow uninterrupted entertainment

**Party Game Appeal:**
- Designed for social gaming experiences centered on music
- Suitable for groups of all sizes
- Affordable price point makes it accessible for party game enthusiasts

---

## Player Count Experience

**Supported Modes:**
- **Local Co-op:** 2 players officially supported
- **Online Multiplayer:** Added in v1.4.0 (experimental) - allows connecting with friends on Steam
- **No support for:** Online co-op, LAN play, or system link

**Multiplayer Features:**
- **Score Modes:** Challenge friends competitively or sing cooperatively
- **Duet Songs:** Both players have different lyrics and notes for coordinated performances
- **Team & Tournament Mode:** Multiple rounds or knock-out tournaments
- **Pass the Mic:** Switch players mid-song for larger groups
- **Companion App:** Free iOS/Android app allows smartphones to be used as microphones; players can search/enqueue songs remotely

**Player Count Flexibility:**
- While officially 2-player local co-op, the "Pass the Mic" feature enables larger group participation
- Current active players: ~11 concurrent (modest but engaged community)

---

## Key Strengths
1. Extensive customization with AI-powered tools
2. Flexible multiplayer modes (competitive, cooperative, casual)
3. Smartphone microphone support via companion app
4. Cross-platform availability including Steam Deck
5. Affordable indie pricing

## Key Weaknesses
1. Technical setup can be challenging for new users
2. AI tools require significant tinkering
3. Microphone synchronization issues reported
4. Early Access means incomplete features
5. Limited online multiplayer functionality (experimental)
