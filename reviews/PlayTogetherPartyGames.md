# Play Together: Party Games

## Game Overview
Play Together: Party Games is a collection of fun and engaging multiplayer games perfect for any party, whether playing locally or remotely. The collection includes action games, turn-based challenges, drawing activities, and other party game formats designed for shared-screen fun.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
- Supports up to 8 players using smartphones as controllers
- No extra hardware needed - players scan a QR code to join
- Variety of game modes including action minigames, strategy, and drawing challenges
- Shared screen experience with phone controllers
- Works both locally (couch co-op) and remotely via streaming/screen sharing
- Free phone controller apps available on iOS, Android, and web browsers

## Fun Factor
Play Together shines through its variety and accessibility. The diverse gameplay modes keep groups engaged, from competitive action games to creative drawing challenges. Players appreciate that non-gamers can easily jump in and have fun. The seamless phone integration removes barriers to entry, and the variety of minigames means there's something for everyone. The game maintains energy through quick rounds and rotating gameplay styles.

## Player Count Experience
- **Supported Players**: Up to 8 players
