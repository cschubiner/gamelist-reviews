# Event Race - Review Summary

Based on available information, here's a comprehensive summary of "Event Race," a recent free-to-play local multiplayer racing game:

---

## Game Overview
- **Developer:** Eventgames
- **Release Date:** September 4, 2025 (full release from early access)
- **Early Access Started:** January 2024
- **Platform:** PC (Steam)
- **Price:** Free-to-play with optional DLC track packs ($2.99 each)
- **Genre:** Arcade Racing / Party Game
- **Player Count:** Up to 30 simultaneous players

---

## Gameplay & Mechanics

**Core Gameplay:**
- Arcade-style racing with retro/old-school aesthetic
- Top-down perspective with both 2D and 3D elements
- Combat racing elements (PvE/PvP modes)
- Physics-based gameplay mechanics
- Colorful, family-friendly visual design
- Inspired by classic arcade racing games

**Controller System:**
- Players can use smartphones as additional controllers
- Joystick support available on most modern smartphones
- Steam Remote Play Together compatibility
- Designed for shared/split-screen local play

---

## Fun Factor

**Positive Aspects:**
- Described as "fun, chaotic, and unforgettable racing competition"
- Emphasizes social gathering and face-to-face interaction
- Community members describe it as "a fun little game"
- Free-to-play model with optional DLC track packs

**Developer Philosophy:**
The developers focus on creating experiences centered around "togetherness, laughter, and companionship through games," celebrating local multiplayer gaming since 2017.

---

## Player Count Experience

**Unique Selling Point:**
- **Up to 30 simultaneous players** - exceptional for a local multiplayer racing game
- Specifically designed for large social gatherings and parties
- Single-player mode available, but multiplayer is the core focus
- Shared/split-screen PvP options

This massive player capacity is Event Race's standout feature, far exceeding typical local multiplayer games.

---

## Overall Reception

**User Reviews (Steam):**
- **95% positive rating** (22 positive, 1 negative out of 23 total reviews)
- Notably, zero reviews came from verified Steam purchasers (due to free-to-play model)

**Professional Critics:**
- **No professional reviews available** on Metacritic or major gaming outlets

**Technical Feedback:**
- Some users reported technical issues with gameplay mechanics
- Developer has been responsive to community feedback
- Requests for Steam achievements and customizable controller bindings
- Active Discord community for feedback and suggestions

**Development Status:**
- Released from Early Access in September 2025
- Developers plan to expand with additional game modes and mechanics
- Two DLC track packs currently available

---

## Summary Assessment

Event Race is a **niche local multiplayer party game** that fills a specific gap in the market for large-group gaming experiences. With its ability to support up to 30 players simultaneously using phones as controllers, it's uniquely positioned for parties, family gatherings, and social events.

The game's **95% positive Steam rating** suggests strong satisfaction among its target audience, though the small review count (23 reviews) and lack of professional critic coverage indicate limited mainstream reach. The **free-to-play model** lowers barriers to entry, making it accessible for spontaneous party gaming.
