# DuckHunt - Missouri Kidz

## Game Information
- **Developer:** Mobile TV Games
- **Publisher:** Mobile TV Games
- **Platforms:** Steam (Windows, Mac), Android (Google Play), iOS (App Store)
- **Release Date:** 2022
- **Price:** $1.99 (Steam); Free apps (Android/iOS)
- **Player Count:** Literally unlimited (any number of smartphones can connect)
- **Unique Feature:** Uses smartphone cameras as controllers via patented computer vision technology

## Overview
DuckHunt - Missouri Kidz is the kid-friendly, paintball-based variant of DuckHunt Missouri. It adds a splash of creativity and color to the traditional carnival shooting game, making it a great game for the whole family. The game transforms your cell phone into a paintball gun using a novel computer vision algorithm that utilizes the cellphone camera to establish where you are aiming at the computer screen - with absolutely no calibration needed.

## Gameplay & Mechanics
The game features custom hand-drawn graphics and animations that add amusement and character to play. Unlike the standard version, this kid-friendly variant uses paintball guns that splash paint, creating fun art effects in the game. There's no violence at all - it's simply a shooting gallery with rubber ducks and clocks.

Players connect their smartphones to the game running on a PC/Mac and use their phone's camera to aim at the screen. The patented technology allows players to just connect and shoot without any calibration process. Multiple players can join simply by downloading the free mobile app and connecting to the same game session.

The kid-friendly theme removes any potentially scary or violent elements from the shooting gallery experience, focusing instead on colorful, playful targets.

## Fun Factor
DuckHunt - Missouri Kidz successfully adapts the classic arcade shooting gallery for modern family gaming. The paintball theme with splashing colors appeals to younger players, and the smartphone-as-controller technology feels novel and engaging. The no-calibration connection makes it easy for kids to jump in and start playing immediately.

The familiar gameplay is intuitive even for young players, while the smartphone aiming adds a modern twist that differentiates it from simple screen-tapping mobile games.

## Player Count Experience
With no limit on the number of players (as many smartphones as can connect), the game is perfect for birthday parties, family gatherings, or classroom settings. Everyone can play simultaneously rather than waiting for turns, keeping engagement high even with large groups of kids.

The free mobile apps mean there's no additional cost per player beyond the initial $1.99 Steam purchase, making it economical for large groups.
